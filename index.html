How does the Waves and Tides application work?


The Waves and Tides Application provides information collected by the Department of Science, Information Technology and Innovation (DSITI) straight to you.


Originally developed for recreational and professional Boaties and Fishermen, the Waves and Tides application allows you to view all information pertaining to the waves and tides of your nearest location, or allows you to search other locations.
